Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Welcome to Relogue ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the Relogue mobile application ("App").

Summary: We collect minimal data needed to provide personalized affirmations. We do NOT collect your location, contacts, photos, health data, or financial information. Your data is never sold to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information to provide our services:

  • First Name: Used to personalize your affirmation experience. You can disable name personalization in Settings.
  • Apple ID Information: If you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address. Apple may provide a private relay email address to protect your actual email.

1.2 Device Information

  • Device Identifier: We generate a random, anonymous device ID to associate your affirmations and usage data with your device. This ID cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Platform: We collect basic device information (iOS/Android) for compatibility purposes.

1.3 Usage and Engagement Data

We collect usage data to improve our services and track your progress:

  • Listening time (total seconds listened)
  • Daily streak and longest streak
  • Last active date
  • Total affirmations generated
  • Daily goal settings

1.4 Affirmation Generation Data

When you create an affirmation, we collect and store:

  • Intention: Your selected focus area (e.g., career, relationships, calm)
  • Questionnaire Responses: Your answers to personalization questions
  • Voice Selection: Your preferred voice for audio generation
  • Generated Script: The AI-generated affirmation text (stored for content moderation and Apple App Store compliance)
Important: We do NOT collect sensitive personal data such as your location, contacts, photos, health data, or financial information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

Data Purpose
First Name Personalize AI-generated affirmations (optional, can be disabled)
Device ID Associate your data with your device; rate limiting
Intention & Responses Generate personalized affirmation content
Generated Scripts Content moderation; Apple App Store compliance
Listening Time & Streaks Track your progress; display statistics in the app
Usage Statistics Improve app features; understand user needs

3. AI-Generated Content

Relogue uses artificial intelligence to create personalized affirmation scripts. Here's what you should know:

  • Your questionnaire responses and preferences are sent to our secure AI processing service (Google Cloud Vertex AI).
  • If you enable name personalization, your first name is included in the AI prompt.
  • Generated affirmation scripts are stored on our servers for content moderation purposes, as required by Apple's App Store guidelines for AI-generated content.
  • AI-generated content is created fresh for each request and is unique to your selections.
  • Audio files are generated using Google Cloud Text-to-Speech and stored locally on your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage (On Your Device)

  • Affirmation audio files
  • Listening history and streak data
  • App preferences and settings
  • Device identifier (stored securely in iOS Keychain)

4.2 Server Storage

  • Generation logs (intention, questionnaire responses, generated scripts, voice selection)
  • Engagement statistics (listening time, streaks, last active date)
  • Anonymous device identifier

4.3 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Server data is stored in a secure cloud environment.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Access to server data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.
  • Generation Logs: Retained on our servers for content moderation and analytics purposes. You can request deletion at any time.
  • Engagement Data: Retained while you actively use the app. Automatically purged after 12 months of inactivity.
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted upon request.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Name Personalization: Toggle off "Personalise Affirmations" in Settings to prevent your name from being used in AI-generated content.
  • Data Export: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  • Data Deletion: You can request deletion of all your server-stored data by contacting support@relogue.app.
  • Local Data: Delete affirmations individually in the app, or uninstall the app to remove all local data.

7. Children's Privacy

Relogue is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our app:

  • Apple Sign In: For secure authentication (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy).
  • Google Cloud Vertex AI: For generating personalized affirmation scripts. Data is processed securely and subject to Google Cloud's Privacy Notice.
  • Google Cloud Text-to-Speech: For generating audio from affirmation scripts.
  • Fly.io: For hosting our backend services (data stored in EU/US regions).
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and subscription management.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States and European Union. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, through in-app notifications. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

Email: support@relogue.app
